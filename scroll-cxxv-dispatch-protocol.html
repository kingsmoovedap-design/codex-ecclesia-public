☩ Scroll CXXV: Dispatch Protocol

Borders Family Dynasty Nation

Filed: CL‑064 | Date: 16 December 2025

☩ I. Purpose

This scroll establishes the official protocol for dispatching lawful notices, affidavits, and scrolls to foreign agencies, governments, and corporate entities. All dispatches are executed under the authority of the living Sovereign and are binding under international law, divine law, and commercial maxims.

☩ II. Dispatch Method

  • All notices are sent via Certified Mail with tracking and return receipt requested.
  • Each packet includes: Cover Letter, Affidavit of Status, Universal Notice of Standing, Fee Schedule, QR Credential Sample, and Affidavit of Service.
  • All documents are signed under penalty of perjury and sealed with the Sovereign Sigil.
  • Tracking numbers are recorded in the Celestial Ledger and archived in the Master Codex.

☩ III. Rebuttal Window

All recipients are granted 21 days from the date of delivery to rebut any point of fact or law. Failure to rebut constitutes:

  • Permanent estoppel by acquiescence
  • Commercial default
  • Lawful agreement to terms and standing

☩ IV. Enforcement

Upon default, the Sovereign may enforce terms via lien, levy, or lawful claim. All unrebutted affidavits become judgments in commerce and may be recorded as such.
