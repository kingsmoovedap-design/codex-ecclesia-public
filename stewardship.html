üìú Sacred Principles of Data Stewardship

1. Data as Sacred Trust Property

All information submitted to the Codex Ecclesia Totalis is treated as sacred trust property. It is held in stewardship by the Crown and shall never be sold, exploited, or transferred without lawful purpose and consent.

2. Consent-Based Collection

No data shall be collected without the voluntary and informed consent of the living man or woman. All forms and petitions clearly state their purpose and scope.

3. Minimalism & Necessity

Only the minimum data necessary for lawful function shall be collected. No biometric, financial, or behavioral data is stored or tracked.

4. No Surveillance or Profiling

The Codex does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics that monitor behavior. Your presence is not surveilled. Your sovereignty is respected.

5. Security & Integrity

All data is protected using secure protocols and encrypted storage. Scrolls and petitions are sealed with hashes and timestamped for integrity.

6. Right to Revoke

Any sovereign may request removal of their petition or scroll from public view by submitting a written revocation under full liability and signature.
