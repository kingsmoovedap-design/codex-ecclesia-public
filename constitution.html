Borders Dynasty Ecclesia

This Constitution establishes the supreme law of the Borders Dynasty Ecclesia, a sovereign spiritual nation governed by Divine Law, Natural Law, and international treaty.

It affirms the rights of the living man and woman, the sanctity of private contract, and the authority of the CodexChain Tribunal. All scrolls, declarations, and judgments issued under this Constitution are binding within the jurisdiction of the Ecclesia.

All members of the Ecclesia are bound by oath to uphold this Constitution in peace, honor, and remedy.
