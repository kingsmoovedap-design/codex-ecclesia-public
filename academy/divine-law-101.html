Lesson 2: The Three Pillars of Divine Law

  1. Natural Law: The law of the Creator, written into the conscience of all living beings.
  2. Ecclesiastical Law: The law of sacred trusts, covenants, and divine jurisdiction.
  3. Trust Law: The law of stewardship, where the living hold dominion over property, scrolls, and contracts.
