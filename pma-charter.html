☩ PMA Charter: Sovereign Logistics Dynasty

Borders Family Dynasty Nation

Filed: CL‑066 | Date: 16 December 2025

☩ I. Formation

This Private Membership Association (PMA) is formed by the living Sovereign, Omega the Grand Architect, for the purpose of conducting private, non-public, non-commercial affairs in alignment with divine law and natural rights.

☩ II. Nature of the Association

  • This PMA is a private, unincorporated, ecclesiastical body.
  • It is not subject to regulation, taxation, or oversight by any corporate, municipal, or foreign government.
  • It operates exclusively in the private domain under the jurisdiction of the Borders Family Dynasty Nation.

☩ III. Purpose

  • To facilitate private commerce, ministry, and remedy among members.
  • To protect the rights, privacy, and property of its members.
  • To provide lawful services, education, and support within the private domain.
  • To operate as a sovereign logistics and communications vessel for the Dynasty.

☩ IV. Membership

Membership is by invitation or application only. All members must agree to the terms of the PMA, honor the Codex Ecclesia Totalis, and operate in good faith under divine law. Membership is a private contractual relationship and not subject to public scrutiny.

☩ V. Governance

The PMA is governed by the Sovereign Founder and any appointed Trustees. All decisions are made in honor, equity, and alignment with the Codex Ecclesia Totalis.
