☩ Ecclesia Market Constitution

I. Preamble

The Ecclesia Market is a sanctified domain of exchange, established to facilitate commerce rooted in honor, truth, and divine reciprocity.

II. Principles of Trade

  • Transparency: All weights, measures, and values shall be true.
  • Honor: Agreements are sacred covenants.
  • Reciprocity: Exchange shall reflect the divine balance of contribution.

III. Jurisdiction

The Market operates under the sovereign authority of the Ecclesia and is governed by its Trade Covenants and Ecclesiastical Law.
