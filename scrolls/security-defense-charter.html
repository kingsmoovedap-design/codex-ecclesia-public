☩ National Security & Defense Charter

This Charter establishes the defensive posture and protective protocols of the Sovereign Ecclesia, ensuring the sanctity of its people, territory, and digital assets.

Article I – Purpose

To provide for the common defense of the Ecclesia, protect the sovereign status of its members, and safeguard all sacred archives and assets from unauthorized interference.

Article II – The Guardian Force

The Guardian Force is hereby established as the defensive arm of the Ecclesia, composed of dedicated protectors of physical and digital domains.

Article III – Cyber & Information Defense

Recognizing the digital archive as a primary sovereign territory, all cryptographic protocols and data integrity measures are declared as national defense priorities.

Article IV – Peaceful Resolution & Neutrality

The Ecclesia maintains a posture of peaceful neutrality while reserving the unalienable right to self-defense under Natural Law and International Customary Law.
