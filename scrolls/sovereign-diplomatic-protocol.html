☩ Sovereign Diplomatic Protocol

I. Preamble

This Protocol governs the formal relations between the Sovereign Ecclesia and other nations, principalities, and jurisdictions.

Article I – Recognition

The Ecclesia recognizes the sovereignty of all living beings and their respective nations, provided such recognition is mutual and based on Natural Law.

Article II – Representation

Diplomatic relations shall be conducted by authorized Envoys and Ambassadors of the Ecclesia, bearing the sacred seal of office.

Article III – Treaties & Covenants

All formal agreements with external entities shall be recorded as Treaties of Peace and Commerce within the Codex Ecclesia.
