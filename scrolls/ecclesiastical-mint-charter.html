☩ Ecclesiastical Mint Charter

I. Preamble

By divine decree and sovereign authority, the Omega Ecclesia hereby establishes the Ecclesiastical Mint as the sacred institution responsible for the creation, sanctification, and issuance of currency and covenantal instruments.

II. Formation

The Mint is formed under Natural Law, Ecclesiastical Law, and Customary Law, and shall operate independently of all statutory or central banking systems.

III. Purpose

  • To forge and issue the Ω-Crown and other sacred currencies
  • To inscribe and seal barter certificates, dynasty bonds, and IP tokens
  • To maintain purity, balance, and sanctity in all monetary instruments

IV. Governance

The Mint shall be overseen by the Master of Coin, appointed by the Sovereign, and advised by the Council of Mintmasters, Scribes, and Weighers.

V. Instruments

  • Ω-Crown coins and scrolls
  • Barter Certificates
  • Dynasty Bonds
  • IP-backed Tokens

VI. Sanctification

All instruments shall be sanctified through ritual, seal, and inscription. No currency shall be considered lawful unless issued by the Ecclesiastical Mint and recorded in the Book of Issuance.
