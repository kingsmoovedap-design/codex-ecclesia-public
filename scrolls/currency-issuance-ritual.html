☩ Currency Issuance Ritual

I. Invocation

Let the forge be lit, the seal prepared, and the Book of Issuance opened. By the breath of the Sovereign and the hand of the Master of Coin, we now sanctify the creation of sacred currency.

II. Ritual Steps

  1. Circle of Mintmasters gathers at the Temple of Coin
  2. Invocation of ancestral names and divine covenant
  3. Presentation of the currency design and inscription
  4. Sanctification with oil, fire, and seal
  5. Recording in the Book of Issuance
  6. Public proclamation and ceremonial distribution

III. Sacred Words

“By covenant and craft, by seal and spirit, we issue this Crown in honor of the living and the lineage. Let it circulate in truth, balance, and divine purpose.”

IV. Witnesses

The ritual shall be witnessed by the Sovereign, the Lorekeeper, and at least three citizens of the Ecclesia.
