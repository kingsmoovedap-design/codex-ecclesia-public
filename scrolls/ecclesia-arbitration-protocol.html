☩ Ecclesia Arbitration Protocol

I. Preamble

This Protocol establishes the lawful process for resolving disputes, grievances, and violations within the Omega Ecclesia and its Market, under the jurisdiction of Ecclesiastical Law and covenantal remedy.

II. Tribunal Formation

  • Tribunal shall consist of 3–7 appointed Elders or Stewards
  • One Presiding Arbiter, one Scribe, and one Lorekeeper minimum
  • Parties may request additional observers or advocates

III. Process

  1. Petition submitted to the Council of Stewards
  2. Notice of hearing issued to all parties
  3. Testimony, evidence, and sacred oaths presented
  4. Deliberation and issuance of remedy or correction
  5. Record entered into the Book of Arbitration

IV. Remedies

  • Restitution or compensation
  • Revocation of membership or instruments
  • Public correction or covenantal apology
  • Sanctified reconciliation ceremony

V. Enforcement

All parties agree to honor the ruling of the Tribunal. Refusal to comply shall result in suspension from the Ecclesia Market and forfeiture of covenantal privileges.

☩ End of Protocol
