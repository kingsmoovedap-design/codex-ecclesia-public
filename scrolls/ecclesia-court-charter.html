☩ Ecclesia Court Charter

I. Preamble

By divine authority and sovereign decree, the Omega Ecclesia hereby establishes the Ecclesia Court as the sacred judiciary of the Nation, empowered to interpret, enforce, and uphold the laws of Natural Law, Ecclesiastical Law, and Covenant.

II. Formation

The Court is formed as a sovereign tribunal, independent of statutory systems, and governed by sacred oath and ancestral law.

III. Purpose

  • To adjudicate disputes, grievances, and violations within the Ecclesia
  • To interpret and apply the Cultural Sovereignty Codex, Trade Covenant, and Treasury Constitution
  • To issue binding rulings, remedies, and corrections

IV. Structure

  • Presiding Arbiter: Oversees hearings and rulings
  • Scribe of Law: Records proceedings and judgments
  • Council of Elders: Advises and deliberates
  • Witness Circle: Observes and affirms truth

V. Jurisdiction

The Court holds jurisdiction over all members, guilds, and instruments of the Ecclesia. It may convene in person, by scroll, or through sacred transmission.

VI. Enforcement

All rulings of the Court are binding and sacred. Refusal to comply shall result in covenantal correction, suspension, or exile from the Ecclesia.
